Secrets of Destroy

One of the secrets of Destroy is living forever. There is a building floating in mid-air, somehow accessible by a teleporter. Once you get into the building, you will never die, even if you come out unless you light the nuclear bomb.

If you want to play the game, download Roblox, create your account, and play it.
